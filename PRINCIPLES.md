# `callisto` development principles

- minimize third party dependencies, and select them dilligently
- minimize obfuscation of critical information
- ensure all relevant data surrounding approvals and signatures is surfaced
- ensure separation of critical key components
- ensure maximum "correctness" when handling any key material
- ensure implementation is maintainable
- ensure adequate commenting and documentation
- follow "best-practices" for ethereum signing-related software
- prioritize 1) security, 2) functionality, and 3) performance
